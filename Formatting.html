Formatting elements were designed to diplay special types of text:

  • <b> - Bold text
  • <strong> - Important text
  • <i> - Italic text
  • <em> - Emphasized text
  • <mark> - Marked text
  • <small> - Small text
  • <del> - Deleted text
  • <ins> - Inserted text
  • <sub> - Subscript text
  • <sup> - Superscript text

Browsers display strong text as bold,and emphasized as italic. However, there is a diference in the meaning of these tags: <b> and <i> defines bold and italic text,but <strong> and <em> means that text is "important".
